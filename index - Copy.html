About ALPINNO GmbH

Founded in 2021 as Monticone Consulting and re-incorporated as ALPINNO GmbH in 2025, we provide technology and innovation services spanning strategy, product development and program management, with a focus on AI. We design, operate and license software and related products, and we turn ideas into protected IP through our AI-Assisted Data-Driven Innovation process. By combining evidence-based experimentation with regulatory and business alignment, ALPINNO helps organisations move from insights to patentable solutions with confidence.

Our Expertise

  • AI-Assisted Data-Driven Innovation

    A structured process that blends AI, big data and expert ideation to generate patentable concepts.

  • Generative AI Strategy & Adoption

    Roadmaps, use-case prioritisation and governance to move from pilots to scalable impact.

  • Agile & Product Development

    Agile transformations and modern product development practices for regulated and high-tech domains.

  • IP & Patent Portfolio Generation

    Invention disclosure support, portfolio development and IP strategy to maximise defensibility.

How We Work

  • Discover

    Innovation brief, insight collection and data analysis to map the problem space.

  • Invent

    Lines of thought, innovation triggers and ideation to generate and refine concepts.

  • Deliver

    Invention disclosure, validation and IP strategy to move from concepts to protected assets.

We validate ideas through a staged path (PoF -> PoC -> MVP -> Scaling) to ensure only the most promising solutions advance.

About the Founder

Pier Paolo Monticone, founder and Managing Director of ALPINNO GmbH (formerly Monticone Consulting), is an innovation-driven executive focused on AI-assisted innovation and Agile transformation.

  • 15+ years of leadership in innovation, R&D and program management across medical devices, AI and high-tech.
  • Head of AI-Assisted Data-Driven Innovation Lab at JTI (2021–2024), raising JTI’s EPO patent ranking from 40th to 23rd.
  • Head of R&D at CorWave (2014–2018), scaling the team from 4 to 45 and supporting $17.1M (2016) and $16.3M (2018) fundraising rounds.
  • Inventor of 200+ patent families and invited speaker at 20+ international conferences.
  • PhD in electronics engineering and MAS MTEC (ETH Zurich), plus BSc/MSc in Electronic Engineering (Politecnico di Milano).
  • PMP, Advanced Certified Scrum Product Owner (ACSPO) and ICAgile Agile Team Facilitation certified.
  • Italian and Swiss; fluent in Italian, English, German and French.
