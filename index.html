About ALPINNO GmbH

ALPINNO GmbH is a Swiss consulting and technology company supporting innovative small companies, startups and scale-ups, as well as selected teams in larger organisations, from strategy through implementation.

Pier Paolo Monticone established Monticone Consulting in 2021, building a commercial track record in AI transformation, AI-assisted innovation, product development and effective execution. In 2025, ALPINNO GmbH was incorporated to develop the company further and expand the established consulting model with proprietary software, licensing and deployment through AIdea.

Consulting, training and hands-on implementation remain core ALPINNO offerings. AIdea complements these capabilities and can also be provided through tailored software-licensing and deployment arrangements. Each offering can be engaged independently or combined according to the client's objectives, internal capabilities and technology environment.

Proprietary ALPINNO technology

AIdea: AI-assisted innovation and invention software

AIdea guides teams through a configurable, evidence-grounded innovation process. It combines proprietary company knowledge, customer insight and public intelligence to create traceable opportunities, ideas, concepts, patentable inventions and invention disclosures.

AIdea is human-led and AI-assisted. Experts shape the brief, choose the methods, review the evidence and make the decisions, while AI helps teams explore more broadly, connect relevant knowledge and document the journey.

Configurable workflow

The workflow can be adapted to the engagement and may include:

  • Innovation brief
  • Project context and evidence
  • Evidence-grounded personas
  • Lines of thought and opportunity directions
  • Innovation triggers
  • Ideas and developed concepts
  • Invention disclosures
  • Preliminary product-design directions
  • Portfolio governance and traceability

Available through tailored engagements

Supported projects, installation and selected beta access

AIdea is offered through ALPINNO-led innovation projects and supported local installations. Selected organisations may also participate as beta users through private-cloud, customer-hosted or tailored licensing arrangements.

A supported local installation may run on a customer-controlled machine capable of local inference. Where required, ALPINNO can work with a specialised infrastructure partner.

Setup, onboarding, methodology training and ongoing support are available according to the client's needs.
