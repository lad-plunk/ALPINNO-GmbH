About ALPINNO GmbH

ALPINNO GmbH is a Swiss consulting and technology company supporting organisations from strategy through implementation.

Pier Paolo Monticone established Monticone Consulting in 2021, building a commercial track record in AI transformation, AI-assisted innovation, product development and effective execution. In 2025, ALPINNO GmbH was incorporated to develop the company further and expand the established consulting model with proprietary software, licensing and deployment through AIdea.

Consulting, training and hands-on implementation remain core ALPINNO offerings. AIdea complements these capabilities and can also be provided through tailored software-licensing and deployment arrangements. Each offering can be engaged independently or combined according to the client's objectives, internal capabilities and technology environment.

The GmbH structure supports continued investment in proprietary technology, broader partnerships and the long-term development of ALPINNO.

Proprietary ALPINNO technology

AIdea: AI-assisted innovation software

AIdea guides teams through a customised innovation process that combines large volumes of proprietary and public data with AI. It turns diverse evidence into structured, traceable innovation outputs.

The journey is configured around each client's objectives, methods and operating environment, while human experts remain in control from the innovation brief and context building through opportunities, ideas, concepts, inventions and product design directions.

Evidence Opportunities Ideas Concepts Inventions

What AIdea supports

  • Guide teams through a client-specific innovation process configured around their goals, methods and governance.
  • Combine large volumes of proprietary company data with relevant public data.
  • Use AI to search, structure and connect evidence while keeping experts in control.
  • Generate, organise and evaluate opportunities, ideas and product concepts.
  • Develop patentable inventions and structured invention disclosures.
  • Maintain traceability from evidence and needs to concepts and inventive features.

Available through tailored engagements

AIdea is offered commercially through ALPINNO-supported innovation projects and tailored licensing or deployment arrangements.
