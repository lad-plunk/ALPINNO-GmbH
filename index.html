About ALPINNO GmbH

About the Founder

Pier Paolo Monticone, founder and Chief Innovation Officer of ALPINNO GmbH (formerly Monticone Consulting), is an innovation‑driven executive with more than 15 years of leadership in research, development and programme management across medical devices, artificial intelligence and high‑tech industries. He founded Monticone Consulting as a sole proprietorship in 2021 and converted it into ALPINNO GmbH in 2025. Pier Paolo has generated over 200 patent families and been an invited speaker at more than 20 international conferences. At JTI he integrated generative AI into innovation processes, elevating the company’s global patent ranking from 40th to 23rd, and previously scaled CorWave’s R&D team from four to 45 people while securing €30 million in funding. He holds a PhD in Electronic Engineering and a Master of Advanced Studies in Management, Technology and Economics from ETH Zürich, is PMP certified, and is fluent in multiple languages.
